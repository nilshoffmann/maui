Maui - the Maltcms User Interface is a rich-client application aimed at researchers working in the field of GC-MS-based Metabolomics.

Maui is based on and complements Maltcms - the Modular Application Toolkit for Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry.

It is available as open source software under the terms of either the GNU Lesser General Public License v3 or, at the licensee's choice, under the terms of the Eclipse Public License 1.0.
